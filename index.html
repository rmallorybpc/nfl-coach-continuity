NFL, 2015 to 2024

The reunion premium

When a player and a former coach come back together, does the player hold his production? This study measures it two ways, the player moving to the coach and the coach arriving to the player, across 8,462 player-seasons. The answer, both ways, is no measurable effect.

Headline finding

Reuniting with a former coach does nothing measurable to the production a player keeps.

The league treats coach familiarity as an edge. Reunited against the right comparison, it is not one. A free agent who signs with a team where a former coach now works retains no more production than any other player who changed teams. A player who stays put and has a former coach arrive on his staff gains nothing measurable over comparable players who did not. Both estimates are small, both sit on zero, and a placebo test cannot tell them apart from noise.

The picture

Two reunion effects, both on zero

What this tracks

A reunion is an event: a player and a coach are on the same team now, were apart the year before, and were together earlier somewhere else, where that coach actually coached the player's position. That splits cleanly into two kinds. In a player-move reunion the player changes teams to a staff with a former coach. In a coach-move reunion the player stays and a former coach arrives. The study measures both, because a reunion can happen from either side, and compares each to the right control: player-move reunions against other players who also moved, coach-move reunions against other players who also stayed.

The question

Does reuniting with a former coach help a player hold his production and his playing time, and does it matter whether the player or the coach is the one who moves?

The approach

An event study over every player-season from 2015 to 2024, with production measured as a within-position percentile and reunions compared only against like players, movers to movers and stayers to stayers.

The data

8,462 player-seasons built from nflverse rosters, snaps, and box scores, joined to a hand-verified 2013 to 2026 coaching-staff table covering head coach through position coach.
