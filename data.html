What is in the panel

Every player-season from 2015 to 2024 where the player has a single, defined team, a measurable production percentile in that season and the one before, an age, and a poolable position (quarterback, running back, receiver, tight end, and the defensive front and secondary). Reunion events are flagged by type; every other qualifying player-season is a control. Movers and stayers are tracked separately so each reunion type is judged against its own kind.

The offensive line

Measured on availability, not quality

Box scores carry no production number for offensive linemen, and Approximate Value, the one metric that does, is not available for recent seasons without a paid subscription. For the line the study uses availability, whether the player held his snaps. Line production quality is not publicly measurable, and this study does not claim to measure it.
